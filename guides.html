Your first day on Ballsnia

  • Catch Pokémon — craft Poké Balls from Apricorns (they grow on trees). Weaken a wild Pokémon in battle, then throw.
  • Open the Pokédex — we've got Cobblepedia in-game; check a Pokémon's spawn conditions before you go hunting.
  • Find them faster — Pokémon show as icons on your minimap (already enabled). Press the minimap keys to look around.
  • Protect your base — claim your area so nobody can grief it. Ask in chat and we'll point you to the claim tool.
  • Learn Create the easy way — hover any Create item in your inventory and press W for Ponder, the built-in animated tutorial.
  • Be nice — it's a friends server. No griefing, no stealing. Have fun. 🇧🇦
